2023-2-20

I have finally smoothed out a problem with how the projectile nodes were being created.
at first the problem was the rocks were always spawning in the same location, even after the tower had been moved.
this was because the position3D node was not a parent of the Tower rigidbody, so its translation was not updating

when it was set under the right node the rock were correctly spawning at the end of the spout
the second problem was that the rocks would always shoot in the same direction, regardless of the tower orientation
I still dont know exactly what cause it, but I fixed it but using the rock basis, and using the Spawner basis instead

I figured out why. At some point in troubleshooting I was only setting the rocks global_translation to that of the Spawner
the global_translation is only the origin and not the basis. I changed it so the rock gets the Spawners global_transform
and now it is in the right orientation

Essentially I was confusing global_translation and global_transform
